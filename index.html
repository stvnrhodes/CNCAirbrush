Welcome!

This is our project website for the Spring 2012 semester of ME102b, the Senior design course in mechatronics at UC Berkeley. For our project, we designed and built a 5-Axis CNC Airbrush Painter. The painter can be used to paint 2D and 3D objects, such as shoes, prototypes, or novelty items.
